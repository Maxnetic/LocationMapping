Here is a short way to get your own personal JSON File from your googlemail oder google+ Account

1. Go to your Account:
2. Click on your Profile Picture in the top right corner
3. Click on Account (or "Konto")
4. Scroll Down to Account tools ("Kontotools")
5. Click on Download Data ("Daten herunterladen")
6. Unmark all Data except for Location History ("Standortverlauf")
7. Click on next, follow the steps and choose your Dataformat and way of download.

Now you can Import your JSON Data into Processing!
